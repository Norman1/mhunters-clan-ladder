01JOINING THE LADDER

#

The ladder is open to every member of the clan. Sign up from the ACTIONS page — the form opens a pre-filled GitHub issue, and Norman, the ladder bot, applies it within minutes.

New players enter the standings immediately at a 1000 rating — Cobalt League, rank Recruit — with a 0–0 record. The first game follows on Norman's next matchmaking run, within ~2 hours.

02GAME CAPS

#

Your game cap is how many ladder games you can have running at once.

  • 0 — paused. You keep your rating and are listed under INACTIVE PLAYERS until you raise the cap again.
  • 1–3 — the allowed range. Default is 2, max is 3.
  • Change it any time from the ACTIONS page.

03MATCHMAKING & THE BOT

#

Norman runs every 2 hours — each run he creates new games between available players and referees the finished ones.

  • Pairing prefers active vs active. Inactive players are still matched — at lower priority, usually against other inactive players — in redemption games.
  • A created game waits in its lobby. If it sits 3 days without both players joining, the bot deletes it and every player who hadn't joined receives a strike.
  • Manually declining a game also counts as a strike.

04RATING

#

Ratings are ELO with K = 40. Everyone starts at 1000.

EXPECTED = 1 / (1 + 10^((OPPONENT − YOU) / 400))

You gain more for beating stronger opponents — swings on the ladder have run from ±1 to ±39.

  • Draws (vote-to-end) change nothing.
  • A surrender or boot counts as a loss.

05STRIKES & INACTIVITY

#
  • 2 consecutive strikes and you are marked INACTIVE — hidden inside INACTIVE PLAYERS on the standings. Your rating is kept.
  • Inactive players get an escalating matchmaking cooldown: 2 weeks, plus 2 weeks per further consecutive strike, capped at 12 weeks.
  • While inactive you are capped at 1 active game until you return.

06COMING BACK

#

There is no reactivate button. Join any game — even a redemption game — and your strikes reset to 0 and you are active immediately.

07MILITARY RANKS

#

Your rank tracks career wins on the ladder. Wins only — losses, draws, and voided games never count, and ranks never go down.
