How to use the ladder

Quick tips

Joining the ladder

You don't have to do anything — every M'Hunters clan member is enrolled automatically (the bot syncs the clan roster every 2 hours). New members start with 1000 ELO and a cap of 2 simultaneous games.

Want in immediately after joining the clan? Open a GitHub issue titled:

Signup: <Your_ID> Name: <Your_Username>

Clan membership is verified — non-members are rejected.

Leaving the clan

If you leave M'Hunters you are automatically retired from the ladder: no new games, active ladder games are voided, and you disappear from the leaderboard. Your history stays. If you rejoin the clan you are reactivated automatically with your old rating.

Update your game cap

Limits your simultaneous ladder games (default 2, max 3, set to 0 to pause).

Update: <Your_Warzone_ID> Cap: <GameLimit>

Example: Update: 1234567 Cap: 3

Opting out

Don't want ladder games? Set your cap to 0 (or use Remove) — you stay registered but receive no games. Ignoring or declining games works too: after 2 strikes the bot backs off, retrying with a single game every 2–12 weeks (2 weeks per strike, capped at 12).

Remove: <Your_Warzone_ID>

Example: Remove: 1234567 (equivalent to Update: 1234567 Cap: 0)

Add a map template (Admin)

Adds a Warzone template to the ladder's random map pool.

AddTemplate: <Template_ID> Name: <Map_Name>

Example: AddTemplate: 1390041 Name: Strategic 1v1

Remove a map template (Admin)

RemoveTemplate: <Template_ID>

Example: RemoveTemplate: 1390041

What to expect

  • The bot checks the ladder every few hours to create or clean up games.
  • Missing two games in a row marks you unreliable: the bot retries you with one game every 2–12 weeks (2 weeks per strike, up to 12) until you join again.
  • You can always re-activate by joining a game or updating your status.
